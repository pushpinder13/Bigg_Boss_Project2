********** Voting Zone **********

A Voting Zone also known as internet voting or e-voting, is a method of casting and submitting votes in an platform through electronic means, typically using the internet. It allows eligible voters to participate in the voting process from the comfort of their own devices, such as computers, smartphones, or tablets, rather than physically visiting a polling station.
